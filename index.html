OUR SERVICES

On-demand Cab services

This is the most popularly used car service, where you get a vehicle on-demand with a chauffeur/driver. Cab services are the immediate alternatives for public transport like city taxis, autos, and buses. The car ride is booked online through an app and the driver picks you up from your location taking you to your destination for a charge that is appropriate for that hour of the day.

Self-drive rental car

Here you get a vehicle on rent to suffice travelling needs but without the driver. The company provides you with a well-maintained car of your choice for a certain period against rent. You can rent it for a few days, every month, or just a day. Different packages determine the rent as per the car model, the duration, and other vehicular features.

Intercity Car Rental

Intercity car rental services allow you to rent a vehicle from your city to another and vice versa. These can be self-driven cars or ones with drivers, as per your preference.

Outstation Car Rental Services

Suppose you are vacationing in a different city and you require a means of conveyance that you can access 24x7. Outstation car rental services usually run a pan-India network, where the company has the connections to provide you with a vehicle in a city other than yours for any period.
